- At start of game, non-participating channel members get quieted.
- When a player dies, they get quieted.
- At night, channel gets set to +m.
- Anyone that joins mid-game is auto-quieted.
- When someone joins the channel, their hostmask is added as a key in the
  player dict.
- When someone leaves the channel, their hostmask is removed from the keys in
  the player dict.
